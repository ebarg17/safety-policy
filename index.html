Privacy Policy for Steady (Beta)

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Welcome to Steady, a space designed to offer a supportive experience while collecting only the minimal information necessary to provide core features. Your privacy matters deeply to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using the Steady app.

🔐 Information We Collect

Data Collected? Details
Email address ✅ Required to create an account Used for account authentication and secure sign-in.
Username and Name ✅ Optional If you choose to create a username and provide a name, it may be visible to other users if you choose to connect or share within the app.
Identity ✅ Optional Used only to personalize how Steady addresses you and tailor the tone of support.
Journal entries ✅ Optional Stored securely so you can revisit, edit, or continue your reflections over time.
Conversation history ✅ Collected Saved to allow continuity and personalization within the Steady chat experience.
Push notification token ✅ If you opt in Used only to send supportive reminders or prompts.
App usage / analytics ❌ No tracking or analytics Steady does not currently use third-party analytics or tracking tools.
Sensitive health data ❌ Not collected Steady does not collect medical records or clinical health information.

🤝 Connecting With Others

Steady may allow users to connect or share activity with others within the app. Participation in these features is entirely optional.

If you choose to create a username or connect with others, limited profile information (such as your name and username) may be visible to those users.

❌ What We Don’t Do

  • No third-party trackers
  • No advertising
  • No selling or sharing personal data with marketers
  • No access to contacts, camera, or location

📬 Push Notifications

If you opt in, Steady may send supportive reminders and check-ins. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

🗄️ Data Storage

Account information, journal entries, and other app content are stored securely using Supabase, a managed database and authentication provider.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information and limit access to authorized systems only.

🧼 How to Delete Your Data

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing:

hello@hellosteady.com

✉️ Questions?

Steady is currently in beta, and your privacy matters deeply to us. If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at hello@hellosteady.com.
