Steady Safety & Child Protection Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Steady is committed to providing a safe, supportive environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior involving child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE).

Prohibited Content

Users may not post, share, or engage in:

  • Any content involving sexual exploitation of minors
  • Any attempt to solicit or groom minors
  • Any content that promotes or enables harm to minors

Reporting & Enforcement

  • Users can report harmful or inappropriate content within the app
  • Reports are reviewed promptly and may result in account suspension or removal
  • We cooperate with law enforcement when required

Prevention Measures

  • We monitor platform activity to detect and prevent abuse
  • We enforce strict community standards
  • We design our platform to prioritize user safety
